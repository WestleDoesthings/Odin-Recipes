Chicken Alfredo

A delightfully filling dish that will leave any family, friends, or just yourself pleased, Chicken Alfredo is easy to make and simple to garnish as well. Served well with salad or your choice of vegetables, this dish can make any evening a night out on the town within your own home!

Recipe

for 6 servings

  • 1 1/2 chicken breast, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 16 oz penne pasta, cooked
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese

    • Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper

Preparation

  1. In a pan over heat begin to melt 2 tablespoons of butter, once butter is melted add cubed chicken breast.
  2. Season Chicken breast with salt, pepper, oregano and basil. Let it cook for 8-10 minutes or until fully cooked. Once ready remove chicken from pan and set aside.
  3. Begin boiling water, once boiling cook penne pasta for 8-12 minutes or until al dente.
  4. Now to begin the sauce, in the same pan melt 2 tablespoons of butter, add garlic and cook until soft
  5. Add half of the flour to the mixture, and stir well, then add in the rest of the flour and continue stirring.
  6. Pour in milk in small amounts, stirring well until sauce is begins to thicken.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil and stir well.
  8. Add parmesan cheese and melt
  9. Pour sauce over cooked noodles, add chicken, garnish with herbs and parmesan, and enjoy!
